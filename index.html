Neumann János

Short english summary

His life

John von Neumann born in 1903.12.28. in Budapesten. He passed away on the 8th of February in 1957 in Washington, USA. He moved to the US after the 2nd world war started and he became an official citizen there in 1937. He joined the fight against the Germans but instead of fighting he was needed for his inteligence.

His work

After the war he started to work with math about sets. In 1944 he was working on a program that stores the data on a faster storage wheere the data constantly changed, It was the RAM. They started using it after 1952. Neumann found out that computers can do more comlex and longer math problems without a mistake not like any human.

His principles

The computers buildup was one of his biggest principle and it was about a controller, memory, logic component, and out-, input parts. He thought about using programs instead of hardware for each and every work.
